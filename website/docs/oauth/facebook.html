Facebook & Instagram OAuth Setup

Set up Facebook App for posting to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts. This guide walks you through the complete process step-by-step.

Before you start: You'll need a Facebook account and access to the Facebook pages you want to manage. For Instagram, you'll need Instagram Business accounts connected to Facebook Pages.

Step 1: Create Facebook App

  1. Go to developers.facebook.com
  2. Click "Create App"
  3. Select "Business" as your app type
  4. Enter your app details:
    • App name: Your company name + "Social Manager" (e.g., "Acme Social Manager")
    • App contact email: Your business email

Step 2: Configure Use Cases

When prompted to add use cases, select these essential options:

✅ Required Selections:
  • Authenticate and request data from users with Facebook Login
  • Manage everything on your Page
Optional but Recommended:
  • Create & manage app ads with Meta Ads Manager (for running ads)
  • Access the Threads API (for Threads integration)
  • Embed Facebook, Instagram and Threads content (for displaying content)

Step 3: Business Portfolio (Optional)

When asked about connecting a business portfolio:

  • Select "I don't want to connect a business portfolio yet" if you're just getting started
  • You can always add this later when you're ready for production

Step 4: App Review Requirements

The system will show publishing requirements:

  • Business verification: Required for live access to Facebook Pages
  • App Review: Required for production use
Development vs Production: During development, you can test with your own Facebook pages without completing business verification. Complete these steps when you're ready to go live.

Step 5: Get Your Credentials

After creating your app, you'll need to gather your credentials:

App ID

  1. From your App Dashboard, the App ID is displayed prominently
  2. Copy this number - you'll need it in ghst_

App Secret

  1. Go to Settings → Basic in your app dashboard
  2. Find the "App Secret" field
  3. Click "Show" and enter your Facebook password
  4. Copy the App Secret - you'll need it in ghst_

Step 6: Configure Redirect URLs

  1. In your Facebook app, go to Products → Facebook Login → Settings
  2. In the "Valid OAuth Redirect URIs" field, add: 
    https://yourdomain.com/api/oauth/callback/facebook
  3. Replace yourdomain.com with your actual domain
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Step 7: Configure ghst_

Now add your Facebook credentials to ghst_:

  1. Log into your ghst_ dashboard
  2. Go to Settings → OAuth APIs
  3. In the Facebook section, enter:
    • Facebook App ID: Your App ID from Step 5
    • Facebook App Secret: Your App Secret from Step 5
  4. Click "Save Settings"

Step 8: Test the Connection

  1. Go to Accounts in your ghst_ dashboard
  2. Click "Connect Facebook Account"
  3. You should be redirected to Facebook for authorization
  4. Grant permissions to your Facebook pages
  5. You should be redirected back to ghst_ with your accounts connected

Instagram Integration

Instagram integration works through Facebook Pages:

Requirements for Instagram:
  • Instagram account must be a Business Account
  • Instagram Business Account must be connected to a Facebook Page
  • You must be an admin of the connected Facebook Page

Converting to Instagram Business Account:

  1. Open Instagram app on mobile
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap "Switch to Professional Account"
  4. Choose "Business"
  5. Connect to your Facebook Page

Production Checklist

Before going live with client accounts:

  • ✅ Complete business verification in Facebook Business Manager
  • ✅ Submit app for review if using advanced permissions
  • ✅ Test posting to your own Facebook Page
  • ✅ Test Instagram Business Account posting
  • ✅ Verify webhook endpoints are working
  • ✅ Set up proper SSL certificate on your domain
Rate Limits: Facebook has strict rate limits for API calls. ghst_ includes automatic rate limiting, but be aware of Facebook's policies around automated posting.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

"Invalid Redirect URI" Error

  • Verify the redirect URL exactly matches what's in your Facebook app settings
  • Ensure you're using https:// (SSL required)
  • Check for trailing slashes or extra characters

"App Not Approved" Error

  • This happens when trying to connect pages you don't admin during development
  • Use your own Facebook pages for testing
  • Complete business verification for production use

Instagram Account Not Showing

  • Verify Instagram account is set to "Business" not "Creator"
  • Ensure Instagram is properly connected to a Facebook Page
  • You must be an admin of the connected Facebook Page
